I am studying food quality of Acartia tonsa by growing the diatom Thalassiosira oceanica in 1 nm deplete and 100 nm replete iron media and mixing these two algae together to create a gradient of iron concentrations. I will be seeing at which concentration of iron the copepods begin to show signs of iron limitation through parameters such as egg production.
