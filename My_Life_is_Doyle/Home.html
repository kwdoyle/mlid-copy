My name is Kevin Doyle. I am a recent graduate of Stony Brook University. I graduated in 2013 with a degree in Biology and have returned to obtain my masters degree in the department of Ecology and Evolution. I have done research in Baines’ Lab of Aquatic Ecology and continue to work there while I pursue my masters. My scientific endeavors are discussed in the science section of the site.


My current focus is on food quality regarding nutrient limitation. I am observing the effect of low iron on the copepod Acartia tonsa. Specifically, I will be determining the specific concentration of iron where these copepods begin to show signs of limitation.


I have also lately developed an interest in Evolution and Genomics and will be attempting to pursue opportunities in these areas as well.
