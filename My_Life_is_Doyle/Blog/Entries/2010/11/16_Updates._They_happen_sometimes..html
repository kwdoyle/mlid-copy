Updates. They happen sometimes.

November 16, 2010

 
 

Hello there, everybody. I just realized haven’t really updated, per say, recently. Nothing really all too exciting has happened lately, so I guess that’d be a good reason behind this. Our band concert is soon though, that’s something. And I had the honor of listening to the ornate orchestra that Nicole’s a member of the other night on the very same stage whereupon we will having our band performance on Thursday, November Eighteenth at Eight O’Clock Post Meridian with ticket prices being Five (5) Dollars for students. (If I haven’t made my point clear, then let me explain in more general terms: you should come.)


And now I’m currently sitting here, awaiting my resplendent journey to class in about an hour while listening to an album which feels like ages ago that I’ve listened to. And I just bought it towards the end of summer, which also feels like ages ago… It invokes some self-established notions that I’ve placed into the songs, those of which bring about some forlorn feelings. But they’re not as omnipresent as they previously were. I’m on to bigger and better things, if you will. Things seem like they might be heading in a nice direction currently in this respect, which is a nice change from the aforementioned formerly present feelings.


It’s also getting to the point now where I just want this semester to end. I know I’m not the only one who feels this way; I’ve heard so many people say the same. While I’m enjoying (most of) my current classes, I’m just awaiting winter break. We’re traveling to Florida again to visit The Fam, which will be fun. Getting to see everyone again will be nice. And also I have to fit in guitar lessons and a winter session class during then. We’ll see how that goes…


You know, I’ve also just realized that it feels good to write sometimes, even if there’s nothing really to write about.

I guess that’s why I have this blog in the first place! My investment in this domain name was not for naught after all!
