One last statement

August 13, 2010

 
 

My heart's been broken. But not in the normal "you'll get over it" sense. This is worse than that. I don't think I'll be able to love anyone the way I did for a long time. I don't want to have that close of a relationship with anyone for quite some time, if ever again. I can't trust people. I don't want to gamble with my emotions anymore.


I've lost my best friend. Or, at least, how she was to me. And that in itself was a lie, which makes it all the more worse. Knowing I’ll never talk to you again, at least in the same way as we once did…


I don't think I've ever been this sad before. Losing someone who meant this much hurts so much.


It seems like a tragic occurrence that I actually did ever meet you in the first place. To experience what true love would be like, only to now have life be all “HA HA you’ll never find that again! Now you’ll just have to settle for something less than that!” Which coincides with me not wanting to try and pursue this kind of relationship with anyone else.


Cause I know I won’t find it again. Despite this all being fake, it never seemed so to me. And the advice of “ooohhh, there, there; you’ll fine someone else; you’ll be okkkk :333333333 <3” angers me a bit, truthfully. I know it’s the only positive sympathetic advice anyone can give, but still. Simply saying that makes it seem like you don’t understand any of this.


And I know you don’t care; you really don’t. You won’t be able to understand how I feel. I can’t blame you for this. It’s just how things are.


I guess things really did have to end this way after all, as devastating as that is.

 

I… I think I’m …I’m ok. I’m begining to realize that you wouldn’t have been a good match in that sense. I’m looking foward now to finding someone …new …to fill that place.


I don’t want to lose you as a friend now.


…I just hope we’ll be able to come back from this.

8/23/10
