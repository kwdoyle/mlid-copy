New site theme!

September 5, 2010

 
 

As you may have noticed, I just changed the theme of my blog. I was growing tired of the dark, dismal “darkroom” layout that I had before. This layout makes my rather seemingly meaningless posts seem more …professional. However, changing the theme wasn’t as easy as I had hoped. I selected the new theme from the happy drop-down menu, whereupon the theme of the main page had then changed to fit my selected choice. It wasn’t until I decided to check on my actual entries when I saw the horror of my carefully planned out layout of the text boxes and other miscellaneous things had all been REARRANGED. I had fun moving things around in EVER ENTRY I’VE EVER MADE. Now I know I’ll be in for an even more exciting time if I ever want to change the theme again!


…I’ve used the word “theme” way too many times in that paragraph.
