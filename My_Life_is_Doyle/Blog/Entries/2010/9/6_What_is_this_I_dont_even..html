What is this I don’t even.

September 6, 2010

 
 

So yeah, I just finished watching a few old episodes of The Office, regarding Jim and Pam’s relationship, and now I’m doubting myself. Yet again.

I dunno… maybe I’m feeling this way because after watching it, I feel more like “Shit. That could have been us,” and I naïvely feel that things could turn out that way, but, alas, it’s not. Because this is real life.

I also have to take my current feelings into account. I’m not really sure I even feel that way anymore, seeing as how our relationship is included in the many “horrible, flaming plane crash[es] [that happened] over the summer.”

I should just move along; things may be looking up, after all. I have to accept the sad fact of what happened and be done with it.


Let’s just hope there’s a lot more exciting things to happen here at school. Band’s already looking to be enjoyable. My friend from high school (and by that, I mean that she’s actually still in high school) is in the band with me. That makes it even more enjoyable, seeing as I already know someone there. And as it turns out, she has a blog too!

The band director also really reminds me of Mr. Peter Randazzo: my band director from high school. They’re friends, so it makes sense that they have similar characteristics.


…


Y’know, this blog is more like an “online journal” or sorts. I haven’t been using it to write interesting things all that much, but just recently I discovered the hi-larious blog that is Hyperbole and a Half, since I’m on a blog-linking spree. I feel like I’ve been inspired to write more often here after discovering it. She is definitely a good writer and her stories are oh so interesting. Too bad mine aren’t oh so interesting.


But who knows what kind of crazy adventures I’ll have to write about in the future!




Update:

Alright. Maybe I’m not truly over all of This. …I really shouldn’t be left alone with my thoughts for too long; I start reevaluating everything over and over again.

Now I kind of feel …alone… and cold.


“Four… three… two… one, I’m letting you go.”


If only it were that easy…


…


I am awfully sorry you must read about this constantly, but I can’t stop writing about it because it BOTHERS ME SO MUCH. And this serves as my semi-hidden online vent-space. So deal with it.



9/8/10 Update:

I do find the irony in me telling you to deal with this, as I myself should be the one dealing with it.
