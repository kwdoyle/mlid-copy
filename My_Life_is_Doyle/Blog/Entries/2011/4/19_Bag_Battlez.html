Bag Battlez™

April 19, 2011

 
 

A few weeks back, I was eating dinner at The Greatest Place on Earth: Wendy’s, where a new toy sensation that will be sweeping the nation was brought into existence. It is called Bag Battlez. You take a paper lunch bag and fill it with miscellaneous object (e.g., trash) and then CRUSH the bag down to create your very own Baag (an official name hasn’t been decided on yet. Or will ever be.)


Once you crush your Bag to create your very own one-of-a-kind Baag, you can then battle them with your friends! No two bags will ever be the same; try to collect them all!! This is going to be the next Beyblades or something.


The Bags will be sold in packs of 5 paper lunch bags of varying colors for $10.99.
