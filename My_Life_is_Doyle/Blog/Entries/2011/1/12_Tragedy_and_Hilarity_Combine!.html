Tragedy and Hilarity Combine?!

January 12, 2011

 
 

Earlier this morning after logging onto my precious MacBook™, I perform my usual routine. Part of this includes checking my Mail, which I do, and I see that Nicole (who now is large and in charge of this here blog) has some new blog posts in my RSS feed—hooray! I proceed to read them, ending on the most recent one being titled: I HAD A DREAM I WAS OBESE, among other, many, posts of obscure pictures that at the time did not seem strange to be posted on Nicole’s blog. (I was still reeling from the intense Snowventure of this morning, freeing our driveway from the chilling grip of the recent Snowpocalypse, so forgive my misjudgment.)


So, I see she is online as well, i.e., on AOL Instant Messenger (iChat for me cause Macs rox my sox) and I decide to playfully initiate the conversation based upon the aforementioned blog post:


Me

so how's that whole being obese thing going for you?

Nicole

ive been obese since age 9, its not going well xD

Me

lolol; I can't say that I've ever had a dream where my body size had become overexcessivly large before

what's that even like? are you imagining (aka dreaming) that and you are all ABDHEJFJD

Nicole

well both my parents struggle with weight so i do everything in my power not to

Me

well I really don't think that's an issue you have to worry about

Nicole

considering i hardly eat, nope xD

im just not a hungry person

Me

but food tastes good!

Nicole

i know



And then we continued to discuss matters pertaining to food, The Universe, and giving ProTips.

The conversation seemed to start off just fine with us jokingly discussing the absurdly made post about having fat dreams. Nothing seemed out of the ordinary, nothing at all.


Much later in the day, after night had fallen over the softly fallen snow of early morn, we started discussing blogs. Me being the flashy showoff that I am </hyperbole>, I sent Nicole a screenshot of my Mail’s RSS feed and how I was able to read her blog posts through there. After viewing the image, she exclaimed in a capslock-fuled fury:


Nicole

WHAT THATS NOT MY BLOG LMAO


to which I replied:


Me

it's the RSS version!1!

[…]

Me

whereupon I can read The Entries



Oh how blissfully unaware I was. And this proceeded to happen:



Me

but w8 where is the other posts?! the "obese dream" one?

Nicole

dude

on tumblr you can change your urls

so i changed mine

and youve been subscribed to the new person who took my url

Me

WHAT

Nicole

YOUVE BEEN STALKING THIS GIRL WHOSE NAME IS GABRIELLA

Me

WOAH

WOAH

[…]

Me

MIND BLOWN; I HAD NO IDEA

Nicole

HAHAHAHAHA DID YOU REALIZE THATS NOT WHAT I NORMALLY TALK ABOUT

Me

so the obese dream wasn't you?!?!?!

Nicole

WHAT OBESE DREAM

Me

OH FUCK



NEEDLESS TO SAY, I felt like The Noob. Luckily this all went down “so well” too! Nicole could have very well been all “GTFO” at me first thing in the morning, leaving me very confused! It is a good thing no angrystrife arose due to this (despite my relentless “OMG I AM SO SORRY”s and “SHITSHITHISHITHISHITHISTHIOMGSHIT”s)


But now after all is said and done, we both now have quite the hi-larious story to tell! …Which is exactly what I am doing right now!


Oh yeah, and she is also never going to let me live this down. As can be seen here. Among all the other, um, incidents that happened during today’s conversation.
